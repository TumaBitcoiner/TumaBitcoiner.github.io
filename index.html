Welcome!

Hi! Tuma Here. I'm an engineer and open-source reporter, writing about open-source development.

I started my Bitcoin journey in 2021.
Since then, I've been fascinated by the idea of a decentralized currency and the technology behind it. I began learning about Bitcoin's underlying principles, such as cryptography, peer-to-peer networking, and consensus mechanisms.

I mainly followed the ecosystem as a viewer, reading articles, watching videos, and listening to podcasts. However, I recently started my own career in the world of Bitcoin and open-source working for BTC++ , one of the most important conference organizer in the Bitcoin space.

In particular, I started working as an open-source reporter for the BTC++ Insider Edition, a newsletter that covers the latest news and developments in the Bitcoin ecosystem.
Moreover, I also started contributing to Bitcoin Optech one of the longest-running open-source Bitcoin newsletters.
Finally, I also have my own newsletter called Inside Bitcoin Code, a weekly appointment in which I explain the codebase of Bitcoin Core to Bitcoin enthusiasts that know nothing about programming.

My goal is to continue contributing to the Bitcoin ecosystem as a writer and open-source specialist.
If you think that my work is interesting and you want to collaborate with me, don't hesitate to reach out!
